(*) = Optional

The onset must be a plosive, fricative, affratate or a plosive and a fricative.
Plosives: p t k p' t' k'
Fricatives: f v 0 6 s z S Z x y
Affricates: tS dZ kx
Liquids: l r j
Other: w M
Plosive and fricative: tf dv ks gz...

The nuclius must ve a vowel, a dipthong, or a nasal.
Vowels: i u e o a A
Dipthongs: iu ie io ia ui ue ua ei eu eo oi oe oa oi oe oa ai au ao
Nasals: m n N
